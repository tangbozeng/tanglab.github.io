Join the Lab
Our lab at NTU studies fungal infection biology, effector regulation, spatial immune architecture in plants, and plant immunity under changing environmental conditions.

We welcome inquiries from NTU undergraduate students interested in Final Year Projects (FYP), NTU MSc students seeking research training, as well as PhD students and postdoctoral researchers interested in plant immunity, plant–microbe interactions, spatial biology, and genomics.

Prospective students and researchers are encouraged to send a short email including their CV and research interests.
